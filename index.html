About

Dominical is the perfect location for you to spend your next vacation. Surround by beautiful natural lamdscapes, you will experience different cultures and traditions, enjoy nature-oriented tours, and adventure activities that involve the air, land, and the sea.

It is 35km away from Sn Isidro de El General, through a paved road that runs south across the El General Valley and from which you can enjoy wonderf panoramic views. It is also possible to reach Dominical from Quepos in about a one hour's drive.

Dominical is a long beach of brown sedimentary sand, and it has been honored with the distinction of the eco-label Blue Flag. It is the only beach in Costa Rica that is suitable for surfing all year round, always under excellent conditions. However, if you wish to go for a swim, to be careful since dangerous currents are common in this area.

Shielded by rock formations that make it a very particular beach, ideal for surfing, Dominical is the perfect site for you to start your exploration of the Costa Rica South pacific.

All of our instructors are experienced surfers that frequently compete in the local surf competitions and have travelled to some of the best surf destinations throughout our beautiful surfing paradise in Costa Rica, as well as outside of central America.
